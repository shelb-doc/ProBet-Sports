Responsible Gaming Policy

Our Commitment

ProBet Sports is committed to promoting responsible gaming practices and providing a safe, enjoyable betting environment for all users.

1. Gaming Should Be Entertainment

Betting should be viewed as a form of entertainment, not a way to make money or solve financial problems. Only bet what you can afford to lose.

2. Know Your Limits

Set clear limits on how much time and money you spend on betting. Never chase losses or bet more than you can afford. Take regular breaks from betting activities.

3. Self-Assessment

Ask yourself these questions regularly:

  • Do I bet more than I can afford to lose?
  • Do I feel the need to bet with increasing amounts of money?
  • Have I tried to win back losses by betting more?
  • Have I borrowed money or sold possessions to bet?
  • Has betting caused problems in my relationships or work?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, you may have a gambling problem.

4. Tools and Controls

We provide the following tools to help you maintain control:

  • Deposit Limits: Set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits
  • Loss Limits: Set maximum loss amounts over specific periods
  • Session Time Limits: Set reminders for how long you've been betting
  • Reality Checks: Receive periodic notifications about time and money spent
  • Self-Exclusion: Temporarily or permanently block access to your account

5. Age Verification

Underage gambling is strictly prohibited. We employ rigorous age verification measures and will immediately close any accounts found to be operated by minors.

6. Protection of Vulnerable Users

We actively monitor for signs of problem gambling and may reach out to users showing concerning patterns. We may restrict or suspend accounts if we believe a user is at risk.

7. Getting Help

If you or someone you know has a gambling problem, help is available:

  • National Problem Gambling Helpline: 1-800-522-4700 (24/7)
  • Gamblers Anonymous: www.gamblersanonymous.org
  • National Council on Problem Gambling: www.ncpgambling.org

8. Family and Friends

If you're concerned about someone's gambling, encourage them to seek help. Consider using our account exclusion features if you share finances with someone who may have a problem.

9. Financial Resources

Never gamble with money needed for essential expenses like rent, bills, or food. Avoid gambling when under the influence of alcohol or drugs, or when feeling depressed or upset.

10. Our Pledge

ProBet Sports pledges to:

  • Provide clear information about gambling risks
  • Make responsible gaming tools easily accessible
  • Train our staff to identify and assist at-risk users
  • Never target or exploit vulnerable individuals
  • Partner with problem gambling organizations
